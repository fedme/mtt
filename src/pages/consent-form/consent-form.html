Statement of Informed Consent

Please consider this information carefully before deciding whether to participate in this research.

Purpose of the research: To examine the way people make decisions and learn about the relationships between stimuli, actions and rewards.

What you will do in this research: Stimuli (cards) will be presented on the computer and you will be asked to learn the relationship between two pieces of information provided by the cards to earn monetary reward. You may also be asked to fill out short questionnaires.

Time required: Participation will take up to 20 minutes to complete.

Risks: There are no risks associated with participation in this research. If you do not feel comfortable, you can stop the game or skip questions at any time.

Compensation: You will receive $0.50 in compensation plus up to $1.40 in bonus for your performance on the task.

Confidentiality: Your participation in this study will remain confidential. Your MTurk worker IDs will NOT be shared with anyone outside of the research team. However, work you perform on MTurk can be linked to your public profile page. Thus, to maximize your privacy, you may wish to restrict what information you choose to share in your public profile. Your de-identified data may be shared with other researchers and used in future projects.

Participation and withdrawal: Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you may refuse to participate or you may choose to withdraw at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.

How to contact the researchers: If you have questions or concerns about your participation or payment, or want to request a summary of research findings, please contact Angela Jones, jones@mpib-berlin.mpg.de.

Whom to contact about your rights as a participant in this research: For questions, concerns, suggestions, or complaints that have not been or cannot be addressed by the researcher, or to report research-related harm, please contact the Committee on the Use of Human Subjects, Richard A. and Susan F. Smith Campus Center, 1350 Mas-sachusetts Avenue, Suite 935, Cambridge, MA 02138; Email: cuhs@harvard.edu
